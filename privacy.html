Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Introduction

Acquisition Pulse ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

ðŸ”’ Our Core Principle: Your data is yours. We process it locally on your device, never store it on our servers, and never share it with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide Directly

  • CSV Upload: When you upload a Shopify orders CSV, we process the data in your browser (order dates, revenue, COGS, platform, customer IDs). We do not store this file.
  • Google Ads Authorization: When you authorize us to connect to Google Ads, you grant us permission to read campaign spend data. We do not store your Google credentials or campaign data on our servers.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email or support channels, we may collect your name, email address, and message content.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Website Analytics: We may use Google Analytics or similar tools to understand how visitors interact with our website (page views, click-through rates, user flows). This data is anonymized and does not identify you personally.
  • Cookies: We use session cookies to maintain your login state and OAuth tokens (stored securely and locally in your browser).
  • IP Address & Device Info: We may log your IP address and basic device information (browser type, OS) for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To process and analyze your Shopify and Google Ads data to generate profitability insights.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Improvement: To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Processing: Your CSV data and Google Ads campaign data are processed and analyzed entirely in your browser. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers.

OAuth Tokens: Google OAuth tokens are stored securely in your browser's local storage. We do not access or store them on our servers.

Data Encryption: All communication between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption (TLS 1.2+).

No Data Selling: We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Ever.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs: For OAuth authentication and campaign data retrieval. Google's Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages: For hosting our website. GitHub's Privacy Statement

We do not share your data with these services beyond what is necessary for functionality (e.g., Google Ads API calls use your own authorized credentials).

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we have about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics and marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@acquisitionpulse.com.

6. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

GDPR (EU Users)

If you're located in the EU, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to be forgotten
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

CCPA (California Users)

If you're a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)

7. Google API Services User Data Policy

Acquisition Pulse's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements:

  • We access Google Ads campaign data only to provide profitability analysis to you.
  • We do not use your Google data for any other purpose.
  • We do not transfer or share your Google data with third parties.
  • Your Google OAuth tokens are stored securely and only used for API calls you explicitly authorize.

8. Children's Privacy

Acquisition Pulse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@acquisitionpulse.com
  • Website: https://ajd84.github.io/acquisition-pulse/
