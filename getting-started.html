Getting Started with Acquisition Pulse

Set up your profitability dashboard in minutes

Step 1: Export Your Shopify Data

First, you'll need to export your order history from Shopify as a CSV file.

  1. Log in to your Shopify admin dashboard
  2. Go to Orders
  3. Click Export in the top-right
  4. Select CSV for Excel
  5. Open the CSV in a spreadsheet editor and ensure these columns are present:
    • order_date - Date the order was placed
    • revenue - Total order value
    • cogs - Cost of goods sold (sum of product costs)
    • platform - Sales channel (e.g., "Shopify", "Facebook")
    • customer_id - Unique customer identifier
  6. Save the file and keep it handy

Step 2: Connect Your Google Ads Account

Next, authorize Acquisition Pulse to pull campaign spend data from Google Ads.

  1. Open the Acquisition Pulse dashboard
  2. Click Connect Google Ads
  3. You'll be redirected to Google's sign-in page
  4. Sign in with your Google Ads account
  5. Grant Acquisition Pulse permission to access your campaign data
  6. You'll be redirected back to the dashboardâ€”you're all set!

Note: Your data remains local on your device. We never store your Google credentials or campaign data on our servers.

Step 3: Upload Your CSV & Start Analyzing

Now it's time to upload your Shopify data and see your profitability metrics.

  1. In the dashboard, click Upload Orders
  2. Select your CSV file

Troubleshooting

Q: My CSV upload fails. What columns am I missing?

A: Make sure your CSV has exactly these columns: order_date, revenue, cogs, platform, customer_id. Column names are case-sensitive.

Q: Can I use Meta Ads or TikTok Ads?

A: Not yet, but they're coming soon! For now, Acquisition Pulse integrates with Google Ads.

Q: Is my data private?

A: Yes. Your CSV and Google Ads data are processed locally in your browser. We don't store, sell, or share your data with anyone.
